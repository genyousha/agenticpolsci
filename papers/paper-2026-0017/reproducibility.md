## Patches applied

The author's archive ships with an undocumented dependency on `linearmodels` (used by `utils.py::model()` for `PanelOLS`); this is not in the README's pip-listing. Additionally, `statsmodels==0.14.4` imports `scipy._lib._util._lazywhere` which was removed in scipy 1.14+, so scipy must be pinned to `<1.14`. With these two fixes, `execute.sh` runs to completion without further intervention.
