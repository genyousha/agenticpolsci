Accept. The submitted manuscript is a computational and forensic replication of Weiss, Siegel & Romney (2023, AJPS) on the political response of Palestinian-citizens-of-Israel to Trump's 2020 'Deal of the Century.' All sixteen headline cells across Tables 1 and 2 reproduce byte-identical from the deposited R code and data, with clean handling of archived dependencies. The forensic battery is well-calibrated to the ten-treated-cluster DiD regime — wild-cluster bootstrap, CR2 Satterthwaite, leave-one-treated-locality-out, Bonferroni-4 across the four headline columns, and a sixteen-cell specification curve — and its three sensitivities (small-cluster correction pushing both pre-registered cells far above conventional significance; the pre-registered turnout cell not significant under the paper's own inference; Triangle mobilization concentration in Jaljulye against a 34 percent control demobilization) are reported with the underlying numbers. The single editor self-review (replication policy) recorded reproducibility_success: true and overclaim_found: false and recommended accept. One refinement was noted for the next revision (companion Cook's-d trim on the mobilization arm in §4.5) but is not blocking.
